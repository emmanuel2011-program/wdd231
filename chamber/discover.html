About Suleja

Suleja, a city nestled in the heart of the Niger State, embodies the essence of a vibrant and thriving community. Located an hour north of Federal Capital, Suleja boasts a rich tapestry of culture, business, and natural beauty. With the total population of 250,000, and a steady growth of 3% annually over the past five years.
